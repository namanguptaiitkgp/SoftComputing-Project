About Performance Predictor

IIT Kharagpur is one of the premier technical institutes in India. It is recognized worldwide with many distinguished alumni around the globe. Many efforts have been put by the administratives of IIT so as to improve the global rankings. IIT Kharagpur being the torchbearer of all IITs, should take the initiative of improving its quality through the strategic program of enhancing the performance of the student community. This served as an inspiration to our team and thus we went on to create a simple and efficient tool of performance predictor for our project.

There can be several approaches to this problem and we made use of data mining. We use Educational Data Mining (EDM) technique to predict the CGPA and/or SGPA of students of various years. In this project, we have discussed in detail about models such as Support Vector Machines(SVM), kNN(k nearest neighbors), Random forest regression and Neural Networks,which have been applied to predict results.

Our model is currently for IIT Kharagpur but it can be extended to other institutes as well.
